Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Overview

Eat My Bubbles is a personal swim tracking app designed for simplicity and clarity. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and how it is stored.

Information We Collect

The app stores swim session data that you manually enter, such as distance, time, and workout details. This data is stored locally on your device and securely synced to a private cloud database associated with your account.

How Your Data Is Used

Your workout data is used solely to display your history, statistics, and progress within the app. It is not shared with any third parties and is never used for advertising, analytics, or profiling.

Data Storage

Your data is stored in two places:

  • On your device – for fast access and offline use.
  • In a secure cloud database – to keep your data backed up and available if you reinstall the app or switch devices.

Only you can access your synced data.

Third‑Party Services

The app may integrate with optional third‑party services such as Samsung Health. Any data accessed from these services is used only within the app and is not transmitted elsewhere.

Your Choices

You may delete your workout data at any time from within the app. Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data. You may also delete your cloud‑synced data by using the account deletion option within the app.

Account Deletion

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your Eat My Bubbles account at any time from within the app.

  1. Open the Profile tab
  2. Tap Delete Account
  3. Confirm deletion

Deletion is immediate and permanent.

What Happens When Your Account Is Deleted

  • Your profile is removed
  • All swim sessions and dryland sessions are deleted
  • All preferences and settings are deleted
  • Your Firebase Authentication account is permanently removed

No data is retained after deletion.
